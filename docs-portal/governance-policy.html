Docs Governance Policy

Operating policy for documentation quality, review, and release control.

Ownership Model

  • Owner: accountable for technical correctness.
  • Reviewer: validates clarity and operational safety.
  • Approver: signs release readiness.

Review Cadence

  • Weekly quick review for active campaign docs.
  • Monthly full review across all pages.
  • Immediate update after major dashboard behavior change.

Media Governance

  • Screenshot naming: module-step-purpose-date.
  • Video chunks: 3-6 min with chapter timestamps.
  • Retire stale media older than 90 days unless revalidated.

Quality Gate Checklist

  1. Behavior accuracy verified in app.
  2. Terminology consistency verified.
  3. SOP actionable without hidden assumptions.
  4. QA evidence attached (screens/video/log).
